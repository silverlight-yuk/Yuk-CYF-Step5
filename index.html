Delicious Japanese Snacks

Japan is known for its beautifully crafted desserts.

Below are four popular Japanese treats: the fluffy Japanese pancake, the creamy matcha pudding, the chewy and savory-sweet dango, and the soft, marshmallow-like daifuku.

Featured Desserts

  • Japanese Pancake
  • Matcha Pudding
  • Dango
  • Daifuku

Japanese Pancake

Fluffy Japanese Pancakes are soft, airy, and cotton candy-like.

Matcha Pudding

This matcha pudding blends Japanese red bean paste, earthy matcha powder, and creamy milk.

Dango

Mitarashi Dango are chewy rice dumplings coated in a sweet soy glaze. They are a classic festival snack in Japan.

Daifuku

Daifuku is a soft, chewy mochi filled with sweet fillings, most commonly red bean paste.
